Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room? +

You can book a room directly through our Availability page. Simply select your preferred room type and click 'Book Now'. You'll be asked to provide some basic information to secure your reservation.

Can I schedule a physical visit? +

Absolutely. We highly encourage prospective residents to tour the facility. You can select "Schedule a Tour" in the contact form above, or call us directly during working hours.

What amenities are included in the price? +

Your booking includes fully furnished rooms, high-speed WiFi, 24/7 power and water supply, access to study lounges, weekly cleaning, and top-tier security. Some premium rooms also include exclusive access to the rooftop lounge.
